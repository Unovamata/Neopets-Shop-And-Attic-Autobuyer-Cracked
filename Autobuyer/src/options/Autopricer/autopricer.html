AutoPricer Settings
Enable Turbo Mode: Turbo mode enables the AutoPricer to skip multiple wait times that mimick human behavior more closely.
Enable Neon for Shop Wizard: This feature combines Neopets Shop Wizard's searches into a single page.

Neon Extension
Pricing Type: Select an algorithm that matches the pricing style you want for your shop. This is designed to mimic human pricing in the player's shop.
  • Percentage: The AutoPricer will subtract the price by a certain percentage (X%) from the lowest price found in the Shop Wizard.

  • Absolute: The AutoPricer will subtract a specific amount (X) from the lowest price found in the Shop Wizard.

  • Random: The AutoPricer will randomly choose to subtract either a percentage (X%) or a specific amount (X) from the lowest price found in the Shop Wizard.
Random Pricing Percentages: If enabled, the AutoPricer will subtract a random percentage to price the items in your shop. This percentage will be between a minimum and maximum number.
Percentage Pricing Algorithm Type: Choose how you want to format your shop's prices:
  • Zeroes: Prices end with zeroes; Lowest price XX,290 becomes XX,000.

  • Nines: Prices end with nines; Lowest price XX,290 becomes XX,999.

  • Unchanged: Prices stay the same, minus X%; Lowest Price = Price - Price Percentage.

  • Random: Prices formatted randomly using the above methods.

Absolute Pricing Algorithm Type: Choose how you want to format your shop's prices:
  • True Absolute: Subtract a fixed amount from the lowest Shop Wizard price; Lowest Price = Price - X.

  • Random Absolute: Subtract a randomly chosen amount from a range of numbers from the lowest Shop Wizard price; Lowest Price = Price - (Random X).

  • Random: Prices formatted randomly using the above methods.

Shop Stock Page Settings
Submit Prices Automatically After Auto-Pricing Stock: If you enable this option, the AutoPricing process will submit automatically all the stock prices to the player's shop after a certain cooldown.
Import Shop Sales History for Analyitics Processing: If you enable this option, the AutoPricing process will extract automatically all your Sales History data. This is useful to visually quantify the performance of the AutoBuyer throughout time.
  • This process runs every time you load your shop inventory if activated.

  • If you utilize the AutoBuyer process across multiple accounts, the analytics results will merge if you use this specific instance of the extension.

  • Your sales history remains stored in the extension even if you delete it from Neopets' servers.

  • You can delete your sales history in the Analytics tab by selecting "Delete Shop History Data."

  • Your sales history will not be exported to your configuration preset if you do not want to. This can be configured in the "Export Configuration Presets" menu.
