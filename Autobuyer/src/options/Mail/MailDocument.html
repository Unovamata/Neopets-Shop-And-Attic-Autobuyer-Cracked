New Version 0.7.17.15v:

    After much anticipation, version 0.7v is now complete. I sincerely appreciate your patience and support throughout the development process. While there are numerous features yet to be implemented in future releases, it may be some time before the next version is ready. In the meantime, I hope you fully enjoy all that version 0.7.17.15v has to offer.

    Version 0.7 has gotten a whopping 17 feature implementations after its initial release with their respective sub-features, fixes, and improvements, marking the most significant update in NeoBuyer+'s development. The stability and resilience of the extension has improved significantly.

  • Show Your Support: Consider Starring NeoBuyer+ or Donating as a token of appreciation. This is made free for everyone to use after hundreds of hours of hard work, so thank you for your continuous support.


  • AutoBuyer & AutoAttic Improvements: Faster AutoBuying and AutoAttic human-like refreshing.

    • AuyoBuying Scripts Optimizations: Now, each AutoBuying script runs independently. While this might not seem significant, it has boosted AutoBuyer speeds and offers flexibility for future features.

    • AutoBuyer Pause Intervals: Thanks to Chrisli-03, the AutoBuyer now has a highly requested featureâ€”Pause Intervals! You can pause the AutoBuyer at specific minute intervals, thanks to Chris.       In the future, I'll document NeoBuyer+ for easier collaboration. Chris implemented this feature without any documentation at all and that's admirable! So, I'll streamline contributions for NeoBuyer+'s growth in the future.


  • AutoPricer Turbo: More consistent pricing.

    • Shop Owner Frozen Detector: The AutoPricer now detects frozen shop owners, addressing profit loss issues because of these frozen users. Previously, it only parsed the first price without considering actual market prices.
      Now, it selects the next valid price in filtered results and checks if the user has been frozen or not based on how competitive their price is compared to other prices in the SW.


  • Analytics Full Release: Visualize data to manage NeoBuyer+ optimally.

    • History, AutoKQ, and AutoPricer Analytics: This feature now adds charts to multiple menus in NeoBuyer+, allowing you to visualize the performance and progress the AutoBuyer has made throughout your usage time.
      I highly suggest you check out this new feature, as it can really help you find that perfect sweet spot for AutoBuying based on your past purchases.


  • GUI Changes: Better menus and instructions.

    • Warnings & Navigation: NeoBuyer+ has new submenus for a cleaner and more cohesive GUI. At the same time, options that may flag the usage of NeoBuyer+ to TNT have now been highlighted in red.
