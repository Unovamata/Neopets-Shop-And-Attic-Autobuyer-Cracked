Version 0.7.7.2 New Additions

New Version 0.7.7.2:

  • Thank you all once again for all your support!

    Version 0.7 has been one of the most extensive updates for the extension in terms of features. As we look ahead to version 0.8, I am thrilled to introduce some exciting new features in version 0.7.7.2.

  • Show Your Support: Consider starring NeoBuyer+ or sending a Donation as a token of appreciation. This is made free for everyone to use after hundreds of hours of hard work, so thank you for your continuous support.


  • AutoPricer Turbo: The original AutoPricer was quite slow, therefore it has been improved in version 0.7.7.2.

    • Faster AutoPricing: To better mimic human behavior the original AutoPricer had many wait times and cooldowns, making it inconveniently slow. Thankfully, that issue has been fixed with "Turbo" mode!

      This new AutoPricer mode removes all slowdowns for faster AutoPricing. Turbo mode is optional for all users. If you prefer playing it safe, you can stick to the original AutoPricer.


  • Restock List Generator: While version 0.8 is in the works, a new alternative has been created to replace database usage in the mean time.

    • Automatic Restock List Generation: Maintaining an updated database has been challenging due to TNT introducing items constantly in the game. Fortunately, the Restock List Generator helps address the current database issues!

      This tool allows the creation of clean restocking lists with minimal effort. In the future, more formats may be supported if any alternatives arise.


  • Version 0.7 in Restrospective:

    • Haggling Changes: Improved to make haggles mimick human behaviour. Now it is quite difficult to pin-point the usage of NeoBuyer+ based on your haggles alone!

    • AutoKQ: For all your Auto Kitchen Quest needs, very useful for training considering the BD plot is right around the corner!

    • Faster AutoPricing.

    • Restock List Generator.

    • Toolbar Improvements.

    • Stability and Feature Improvements.


  • What is Left for Version 0.7?

    • AutoBuyer Improvements: Currently, all haggling, AuyoBuying, and AutoAttic activities run in a single script. These features will all become their own separate script to improve AutoBuying speeds.

    • History & AutoKQ Analytics: This feature has been broken since version 0.5, but now it is returning stronger than ever!

    • AutoPricer Shop Owner Frozen Detection: If the AutoPricer detects the owner of a shop has been frozen, then it will not count its price as being the lowest in the Shop Wizard.

    • Updating the FAQs.

    • Possible Discord Server.


  • What is to come?

    • Real-time Database Updates: NeoBuyer+ will have the possibility of updating its database in real-time, removing the need of manual Database updates.

    • AutoSniper: For AutoBuying and sniping using the Shop Wizard.

    • AutoStock / AutoSDB: Useful for automatically sending items to your Shop Stock or Safety Deposit Box.

    • AutoBD: For all your Battledome needs.

    • AutoAuctions: For AutoBuying in player's auctions.

    • AutoDailies: To automate Neopets Dailies.

    • AutoBuyer Improvements.


    • I hope you are as excited as I am for all the updates that will come in the future. Thank you once again for your continuous support!
