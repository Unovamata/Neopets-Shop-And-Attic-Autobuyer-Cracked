NeoBuyer+ Tools


Save & Load Configuration
Share Store Numbers to Cycle: If you enable this option, the configuration exporter will include the 'Store Numbers to Cycle Through When Stocked' data.

This option is only included if you are concerned about sharing this information, as users may be able to match your shop's stock with the stores you browse.

This data may be considered potentially identifiable.
Share Buyable Items List: If you enable this option, the configuration exporter will include your 'Restock List' data.

This is not identifiable information.
Share Shop Stock: If you enable this option, the configuration exporter will include your entire 'Shop Inventory' data.

This is HIGHLY identifiable information.

You could potentially get frozen if you share this!

Please proceed with caution.
Share Blacklists: If you enable this option, the configuration exporter will include your 'Blacklist' data.

This is not identifiable information.
Share Neopets Security PIN: If you enable this option, the configuration exporter will include your Neopets' account PIN.

Only share this information between local sessions of NeoBuyer+ if possible.

This is HIGHLY identifiable information.

You could potentially get frozen if you share this!

Please proceed with caution.
Share Attic Last Refresh Time: If you enable this option, the configuration exporter will include the 'Last Restock Time in NST' data.

This option is only included if you are concerned about sharing this information, as users may be able to match your account based on Trading Post lots.

This data may be considered potentially identifiable.
Share Emailjs Data: If you enable this option, the configuration exporter will include the 'Send Email After Purchase Using Emailjs'.

To be clear, this is NOT your email address, this is the data you loaded into the Emailjs fields in the AutoBuyer pages.

This is not identifiable information.
Share Purchased Items History: If you enable this option, the configuration exporter will include your entire 'Purchase History'.

This is HIGHLY identifiable information as it includes your username and purchase dates.

You could potentially get frozen if you share this!

Please proceed with caution.
Share NeoBuyer+ Emails: If you enable this option, the configuration exporter will include your entire NeoBuyer+ Email list.

To be clear, this is NOT your email address or any of your emails in your inbox from your personal email, these are NeoBuyer+'s propietary Emails and nothing else.

This is not identifiable information.
